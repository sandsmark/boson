About Boson

Boson is a real-time strategy game, like Command&Conquer™ or StarCraft™. It is designed to run on Unix (Linux) computers, and is built on top of the libkdegames, kde and Qt libraries.
A minimum of two players is required, since there is no artificial intelligence yet.
