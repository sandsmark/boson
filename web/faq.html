Those are the frequently asked questions (with their answers) concerning boson

Will there be a port to gtk (or any other toolkit) ?

 Why not ? The code is heavily based on qt/kde libraries, but as many people have already asked for a gtk port, I will surely separate boson in the future : the core, toolkit-agnostic, and the GUI part (one for kde, one for gtk..). The main purpose is to have a playable game, though, and this won't be done before 1.0, at best.

What does BOSON stand for ?

 As far as I know, a boson is a sub-particle. Well... the name was chosen in a hurry, and I don't have time to find a better one. Feel free to make any proposal to the boson mailing list

What about Qt 2 / KDE 2 ?

 Presently, I'm using kde 1.x and qt 1.4x for the development. As soon as I will have a almost stable kde2/qt2, I will begin the port to it. (the one I have is still.. well.. not for daily use yet)
Boson 1.0 will need qt2/kde2. That's for sure.
