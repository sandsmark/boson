What is Boson ?

Boson is a real-time strategy game, like Command&Conquer(tm) or StarCraft(tm). It is designed to run on Unix (Linux) computers, and is built on top of the libkdegames, kde and Qt libraries.

A minimum of two players is required, since there is no artificial intelligence yet.
