Boson:

Boson is a real-time strategy game, like Command&Conquer(tm) or StarCraft(tm). It is designed to run on Unix (Linux) computers and uses the QT Toolkit and the QwSpriteField library. A minimum of two players is required, since there is no artifical intelligence yet. Boson is still in very early development and not playable yet.

Boson's Code and Graphics are published under the GNU General Public License.

If you want to know more about Boson, take a look at the other pages.
