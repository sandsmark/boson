Overview:

Boson is a real-time strategy game, like Command&Conquer(tm) or StarCraft(tm). It is designed to run on Unix (Linux) computers, uses the QT Toolkit and the QwSpriteField library. A minimum of two players is required, since there is no artificial intelligence yet. Boson is still in very early development and not playable yet.

Boson's Code and Graphics are published under the GNU General Public License (GPL).

This webpage is viewn best with any browser at a resolution of 1024 X 768 and a color depth of 32 bits.

The best way to ask a question or to be in touch with the boson team is to Contact me.
