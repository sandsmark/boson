Boson:

Boson is a real-time strategy game, like Command&Conquer(tm) or StarCraft(tm). It is designed to run on Unix (Linux) computers, uses the QT Toolkit and the QwSpriteField library. A minimum of two players is required, since there is no artifical intelligence yet. Boson is still in very early development and not playable yet.
