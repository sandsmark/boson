History

(this text was written by Thomas Capricelli <orzel@kde.org>, on february 5th, 2002)

I've started to write boson quite a long time ago. I finally almost stopped developping it at the end of year 2000. (lack of time, and feeling too much alone on the project, that's sadly quite common for open source project).

Then, during 2001, Andreas Beckermann begins to code on boson. He had already worked on the libkdegames library, which implements lot of common stuff for kde games. He then thought he could port boson to use that library, and ended rewriting large parts of boson.
