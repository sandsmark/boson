What is this?

This is the handbook to the realtime-strategy game Boson. It will guide you the way through all the problems coming with the game and will answer most of your questions. NOTE: this handbook is far from being completed. It only has installation and FAQ sections at the moment (and section about scrolling in the game :-)).
