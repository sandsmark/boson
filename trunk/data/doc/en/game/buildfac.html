Building facilities

To build facilities choose the Command bunker and choose your building.
You can see that the building is getting constructed. When it is finished, click with the right mouse button on a free place on the map.
Note:
- You have to build in the near of your other buildings.
- If the building is not available look at the Facilities dependency tree and check if you have all required buildings.
